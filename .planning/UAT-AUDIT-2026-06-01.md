## Prioritized Human Test Plan

### Phase A — Navigation & Shell (Do first)

1. **Header + Footer on every page**
   - Visit: `/`, `/record`, `/submit`, `/entity/ent-001`, `/login`, `/dashboard`, `/mission`, `/faq`, `/policy`, `/terms`, `/privacy`, `/reply`
   - Expected: Sticky header with seal + nav on all. Dark green footer with creed pills on all.

2. **Evidence card click-through**
   - Click any card on homepage or `/record`
   - Expected: Routes to entity detail page with full case file layout.

3. **Pagination**
   - Homepage: click "Next" → URL changes to `?page=2`, new entities load.
   - `/record`: change filters, click "Next" → new results load.

### Phase B — Forms & Interactions

4. **Submit form**
   - Fill all sections, upload a file, submit anonymously.
   - Expected: Success message, form resets.

5. **Login + 2FA**
   - Enter credentials, click "Show" to reveal password.
   - If 2FA enabled, enter TOTP code.
   - Expected: Redirects to `/reviewer`.

6. **Right of reply**
   - Submit a reply at `/reply`.
   - Expected: Success confirmation.

### Phase C — Mobile & Responsive

7. **Mobile filter toggle**
   - Open `/` or `/record` on screen <860px.
   - Expected: "Filters" button appears, opens filter panel above results.

8. **Touch targets**
   - Verify buttons and cards are tappable on mobile (no tiny click areas).

### Phase D — Edge Cases

9. **Empty states**
   - Search for nonsense text on `/record`.
   - Expected: Styled empty card with "Clear filters" button.

10. **Brand consistency**
    - Verify "حملة لستَ شجرة" appears in header, footer, hero, and metadata on Arabic pages.
    - Verify "HLShajara" appears on English pages.
